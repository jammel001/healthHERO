HealthChero — User Guidelines & Safety

Last updated:

About this assistant

This assistant (HealthChero) provides informational health guidance based on symptoms entered by users. It is built by Bara'u Magaji and team. The system uses a trained model and third-party medical references to suggest possible conditions and general advice.

How to use

  1. Describe symptoms clearly in the chat or use the Quick Form (comma-separated).
  2. Confirm suggestions when asked (the assistant may propose canonical symptom names).
  3. Provide optional patient details when requested (age/sex) to improve guidance.
  4. After the assessment you may download a PDF summary.

Important safety notes

  • Not a diagnosis: This tool is for information only and does not replace professional medical assessment.
  • Urgent symptoms: If you experience chest pain, trouble breathing, severe bleeding, sudden weakness, loss of consciousness, or severe allergic reactions — seek emergency care immediately.
  • Children, pregnant people, elderly: If the patient is a child, pregnant, or older than 65, seek direct clinical care when symptoms are concerning.
  • Privacy: Some data (assessments and generated PDFs) are stored for service improvement. Contact admin@healthchero.com to request deletion.

Limitations

The model's predictions depend on the quality and coverage of training data. Rare conditions may be missed. Use official guidance sources for definitive clinical decisions.
